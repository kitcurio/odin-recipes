Angel Hair Pasta

Description

This pasta with broccoli and garlic makes a beautiful side dish or vegetarian main course. If you've got to eat your broccoli, this is a great way to do it!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Broccoli
  • Chicken stock
  • Angel hair pasta

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in large skillet.
  2. Add garlic and cook, stirring often, until soft.
  3. Sprinkle in pepper flakes and season with salt.
  4. Pour chicken stock into skillet and bring to simmer.
  5. Add broccoli and simmer until tender.
  6. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil.
  7. Add pasta and cook until almost tender.
  8. Drain pasta and move to large pot, pouring broccoli sauce over top.
  9. Add parmesan, and stir to combine.
  10. Cover the pot and let stand about 2 minutes.
